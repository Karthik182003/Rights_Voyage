All children have all these rights, no matter who they are, where they live, what language they speak, what their religion is, what they think, what they look like, if they are a boy or girl, if they have a disability, if they are rich or poor, and no matter who their parents or families are or what their parents or families believe or do. No child should be treated unfairly for any reason.

Right to Education: Every child has the right to an education. Primary education should be free. Secondary education should be accessible to every child â€“ every child should receive the highest level of education available to them..

Right to protection: Children have the right to be protected from doing work that is dangerous or bad for their education, health or development. If children work, they have the right to be safe and paid fairly.

Right to Access Information: Children have the right to get information from the Internet, radio, television, newspapers, books and other sources. Adults should make sure the information they are getting is not harmful. Governments should encourage the media to share information from lots of different sources, in languages that all children can understand..

Right to Family: Every child has the right to live with their parents unless it's against their best interests.

Right to Expression: Every child has the right to express their opinions freely and to have a say in matters affecting them.

Right to Health: Children have the right to the best health care possible, clean water to drink, healthy food and a clean and safe environment to live in. All adults and children should have information about how to stay safe and healthy..
